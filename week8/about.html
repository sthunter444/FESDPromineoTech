Cherry Hill Dog Park


One of the best dog parks in Coeur dAlene


Why is this the best park??


  • This park is completely contained for your dog's safety.
  • There are baggies provided for waste.
  • It is a beautiful environment, trees and mountains surround the park.
  • The community of Cd'A is dog friendly and welcoming.
  • There are other activities at this park: a child's playground, tennis courts, a BMX track, a climbing hill, horseshoes, and plenty of open space.
